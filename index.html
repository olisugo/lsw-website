Phoenix One is a side-scrolling 2D platformer set on an attacked spacecraft in the year 2164. Play as Astrid, a genetically engineered soldier, who must hastily traverse the wreckage of the Phoenix One, killing aliens blocking their path, all before the oxygen supply runs out. The game has puzzle-like elements, such as a unique ricochet mechanic, that you must learn to utilise and master under time pressure. Humanity is relying on Astrid to bring back sensitive information gathered by the Phoenix One that is crucial for our survival. Can you ensure the success of the Phoenix One?
