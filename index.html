The Phoenix One has been ambushed and is now drifting through space. Play as Astrid, the last human on board capable of completing its mission, in this side-scrolling 2D platformer. A strange species of alien has taken over the wreckage so you must eliminate them all before your oxygen runs out but there’s a twist… the aliens have shields so you have to ricochet your shots off the ship and debris to hit them from behind. Phoenix One takes elements from the run-and-gun and puzzle genres to create a unique fast paced game. The outcome of the mission is in your hands.
